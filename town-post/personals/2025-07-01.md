### First Update from Me

I have tossed around the idea of adding a town post to Adam's Land for a while
but I was concerned that I would have nothing to write about and that I would
eventually abandon this side of the Land.

I have since changed my mind because I am now truly waking up to the impact of
avoidant and compulsive media consumption in my daily life.

In order to fill the time, keep myself motivated, and document the journey,
I figured it would be fun to write about some of that here and maybe encourage
someone else along the way.
