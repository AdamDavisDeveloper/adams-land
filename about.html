About Adam

Feeling:

Adam had the idea to found Adams Land back in September of 2021. He's been slowly developing this land since then but he's had to take an extended vacation after some events last year.

Adam loves to write code, read books, and create new, creative things! He carries a camera with him everywhere and captures moments that he finds special.
