About Adam

Adam had the idea for Adams Land back in September of 2021. He's been slowly developing this land since then but he's had to take an extended vacation after some events last year.

Adam loves to write code, read books, and create new, creative things! He carries a camera with him everywhere he goes and captures moments he finds special.
